We are Native Architecture

Native architecture has been at the forefront of innovation for the past 5 years and even though we are still considered a young company we have already made a name for ourselves as one of the top three architecture firms in America.

We have designed some of the most renown buildings in the entire world as well as a line of forward thinking housing possibilities. We have even participated in the arts, more specifically we have worked with a select group of artists and designers to help bring immmersive interactive installations to life.

Menu

Native Architecture began merely as an idea between two friends. An idea about creating a architecture/design firm that would push the boundaries of design and challenge the the status quo. We are now a formidable team of designers, architects, and engineers ready to take on whatever comes our way.

After much deliberation the cofounders, Sam and Tatum, decided to make their idea come to life and have never looked back since. What began as endless hours in the library and many sleepless nights turned out to be the foundation that was set for Native Architecture.
